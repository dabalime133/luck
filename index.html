What is Lucky Jet and how does the multiplier work?

+

Lucky Jet is a provably fair crash multiplier game. You place a bet before each round, watch Lucky Joe ascend with a climbing multiplier, and cash out manually — or set an auto-cashout — before he flies away. Your payout is your bet multiplied by the cashout multiplier. If you don't cash out in time, you lose your wager.
